A text prompt acts as a seed for the model which then computes a sequence of words based on probability.

The length of the generated text. A max of 1023 words can be generated - which can take up to two minutes.

Temperature controls the randomness of predictions. When the temperature approaches 1 predictions become less confident and more "creative". Temperature therefore increases the sensitivity to low probability candidates.

Top-k limits the generated words to a select number of words with the highest probability of appearing. Setting the value to 0 removes this restriction.
